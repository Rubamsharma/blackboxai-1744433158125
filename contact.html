Frequently Asked Questions

How can I access UDISE+ data?

You can access UDISE+ data through our official portal after registration. The basic statistics are available publicly, while detailed reports require login credentials.

What kind of support is available?

We provide technical support through email and phone. Our support team is available during working hours to assist with data-related queries and technical issues.

How often is the data updated?

UDISE+ data is updated annually. Schools submit their data during the academic year, which is then verified and published after processing.
